What is Rep Shoes

Rep shoes, are imitation footwear designed to closely resemble popular designer or streetwear branded shoes. They are produced by independent factory and offer a budget-friendly alternative to expensive, high-end footwear. Reps shoes are popular because of its affordable price and stylish design.
